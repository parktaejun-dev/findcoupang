# Privacy Policy for Ref-Check

**Last Updated:** January 12, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Ref-Check ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension handles your data.

## 2. Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.**
* We do not have a backend server.
* We do not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics).
* All processing happens locally on your device.

## 3. Permissions Usage
* **Storage:** Used only to save your settings (e.g., Preview Mode ON/OFF) locally in your browser.
* **Host Permissions:** Used only to fetch and scan the HTML of the search result links you interact with. This data is processed in your browser's memory and is immediately discarded after analysis. It is never sent to us or third parties.

## 4. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via the GitHub repository issue tracker.
